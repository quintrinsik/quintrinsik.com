03 — Typeface

Friendly, with a spine

Wide appeal, not yet everywhere. All three are open-source and free to ship.

Gabarito Display / logo

Bright by design

Regular Semibold Extrabold Black

ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZ · abcdefghijklmnopqrstuvwxyz · 0123456789

Gabarito has the rounded warmth game brands lean on, but more backbone and less nursery than Fredoka or Poppins. It is free under the Open Font License and still uncommon, so it reads as a choice rather than a default. Use Black for the wordmark and big headlines, Extrabold for section heads.

Onest Body / interface

Clear at any size, calm under the brights.

Light Regular Medium Bold

A fresh, neutral humanist sans that stays out of the way in long copy and dense UI. Pairs with Gabarito without competing, and it is nowhere near as worn as Inter.

Geist Mono Utility / data

v1 · #2D5BFF · 100% core

0123456789 · {} [] () · </> · ABCDEF

For hex values, labels, code, and fine print. This is also the deliberate tie to Perpensa, which runs Geist Mono in its Counterweight system. Same mono across both brands is a resemblance only you and your team will clock.

04 — Family resemblance

Sneaky close to Perpensa

Not a copy. A shared idea, drawn twice. Both brands are about separate things becoming one.

Perpensa

Converge · streams meet

Separate lines of thought flow together and leave as one weighed conclusion. Brass core, mono detailing, muted metal palette.

Quintrinsik

Five facets · one core

The same convergence, turned radial and lit up. Five blades, one bright core, full-spectrum energy. Brass core kept, so the two share a heartbeat.

Convergence
Perpensa's meeting point becomes Quintrinsik's radial core. Same concept, different geometry.
Brass → Brass
The core stays #FFB81C in both. Identical warm centre, so they feel related at a glance.
Signal → Pink
Perpensa's signal accent reappears, brightened, as Signal Pink.
Geist Mono
The exact same utility face runs through both systems. A quiet shared fingerprint.

Voice

Plain verbs, sentence case, a little spark. Quintrinsik talks like a sharp friend who happens to be great at getting things done. Bright, never loud for its own sake.
